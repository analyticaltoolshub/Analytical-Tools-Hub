Decision Support

The Right Method for the Right Decision

Some business decisions rely entirely on measurable historical data. Others depend on structured expert judgement. Many important decisions require a combination of both. Analytical Tools Hub brings together quantitative analysis, structured expert judgement, Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM), practical business analytics, and transparent analytical methods so users can choose an approach that fits the decision in front of them.

Data-Driven Decisions

Use measurable demand, cost, sales, inventory, or performance data to calculate results and compare options.

Examples: ABC Analysis, EOQ, Safety Stock, Forecasting.

Expert Judgement Decisions

Structure subjective expertise when decisions involve criteria, trade-offs, preferences, or professional judgement.

Example: Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP).

Hybrid Decision Support

Combine data, risk assessment, categorisation, and judgement to support decisions that are not solved by one metric alone.

Example: Kraljic Matrix.
